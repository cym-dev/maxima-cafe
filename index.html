About Maxima Cafe

Cabanatuan's Premier Coffee Destination by Rona Joy Apuan

Founded by Rona Joy Apuan, Maxima Cafe has become the premier coffee destination in Cabanatuan City, Nueva Ecija. Our cafe offers an exceptional coffee experience with premium espresso, lattes, cappuccinos, and specialty drinks crafted by expert baristas.

Located in the vibrant heart of Cabanatuan City, Maxima Cafe has been serving exceptional coffee and delightful Filipino cuisine since our establishment. We pride ourselves on creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where friends gather, business meetings flourish, and coffee lovers from Cabanatuan and all over Nueva Ecija find their perfect cup.
